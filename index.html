About

Sheethal Sukumar is a dedicated software engineer with over 1.5 years of experience in full-stack development at L&T Construction. In her current role, she has successfully contributed to projects across the tech stack, combining strong technical skills with excellent communication to support users effectively. A Computer Science Engineering graduate from the Government College of Engineering Kannur, Sheethal has a history of active participation in technical and community-based initiatives. Her early experiences include contributing as a web volunteer for the IEEE WiE Kerala Section and leading the women entrepreneurship team for the Venus Campaign.

Sheethal’s journey in tech is marked by hands-on project work, internship experiences, and leadership in college organizations such as ISTE, Robocek, and the SHE club’s website development team. Additionally, she is a member of WomenTech Network and SheThePeopleTV. Her achievements include winning the Best Outgoing Woman Engineer Award 2023 (CSE department), for her academic and leadership contributions, and the Best Student Award in 2020-21. She continues to embrace learning and growth, bringing a commitment to both innovation and teamwork to every project.
